Crown Jewels: How to Actually Survive 嬢王

1. Speed setting: 10.6 – 10.9 (slightly lower than Kaijuu). You need clarity for the staircase flicks.

2. The 0:38 “Queen’s Staircase”: Let your right hand go into autopilot. Focus your eyes on the left hand’s slide pattern. Chunk the staircase into “up → diagonal” groups mentally.

3. 1:10 Throne Room (4-note bursts + jacks): Sync both hands perfectly. Count the downbeats aloud – if you drift here, you'll never recover.

4. Disable MV / Simple mode. The glittering visuals are gorgeous but they stab your concentration.

5. Wrist care: 嬢王 forces wide wrist motion. Stretch before every session. Ice after 5 attempts. Tendonitis is real.
